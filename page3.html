QRCS has provided Medical, Health, Nutrition and Food Security, and Shelter Services to 405,100 individuals during 2016-2015. QRCS has worked in the previous phase with exceptional and very difficult circumstances, and reached the areas that many International Organizations were unable to reach, Such as Taiz, Lahj, Marab, Al-Hodeida and Abyan Gov., where QRCS has a good communication and coordination with authorities and executive unit. QRCS opened and fully function of 15 primary health centers, which were closed, or partial worked through providing Incentive to Health Staff, Essential Drugs, Medical Supplies, Medical Equipment, Fuel, and Water. QRCS also supported in referral cases, training of staff and rising health awareness in community .QRCS had two sup offices in Taiz governorate since 2015 and officially opened an office in Sana'a city in January 2017.
